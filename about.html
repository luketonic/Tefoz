About TeFOZ

background

don't mourn, organise

An independent Teachers Trade Union representing the welfare, socio-economic interests and values of all primary and secondary school teachers of Zimbabwe. TeFOZ was established in 2017 and was registered in 2021. TeFOZ is committed to transform the mind set of teachers to think out side the box

Vision
To be the leading Forum for championing teacher empowerment and improving their status

Mission
To educate, advocate, empower, and protect our membership through efficient and effective management of the Forum's affairs

Core Values
Integrity – we uphold the virtues of honesty and professionalism
Respect - we value each other's diversity and treat all people with the utmost dignity and respect
Excellence – we deliver what we promise and add value that goes beyond what is expected
Responsiveness – we are constantly anticipating market trends and translating them into opportunities and progress, for our members and for ourselves
Innovation – developing innovative ideas and putting them into practice successfully.
